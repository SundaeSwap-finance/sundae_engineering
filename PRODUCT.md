# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Primary audience is CTOs and VPs of Engineering evaluating external engineering partners for critical product and platform work. They are time-constrained technical buyers who scan for credibility, delivery maturity, and risk awareness before taking a first call.

## Product Purpose
Sundae Engineering exists to convert high-intent technical leaders into inbound conversations. The landing page should quickly communicate senior-level engineering capability for high-stakes systems, reduce perceived delivery risk, and drive direct outreach through a clear call to action.

## Brand Personality
Serious, precise, and operationally mature. Tone should feel like institutional-grade technology and financial infrastructure communication: direct language, no hype, no fluff, high signal density.

## Anti-references
Do not resemble crypto or blockchain marketing. Avoid neon palettes, speculative language, token or chain metaphors, futuristic gimmicks, and visual hype patterns that reduce trust with enterprise technical buyers.

## Design Principles
1. Prove competence before asking for trust: lead with concrete operating claims and delivery posture.
2. Minimize cognitive overhead: emphasize clear hierarchy, tight copy, and one primary conversion path.
3. Signal discipline through detail: spacing, typography, and component behavior should feel deliberate and controlled.
4. Prefer operational clarity over marketing flourish: use plain, defensible language and concrete service framing.
5. Keep conversion always available: primary contact action remains visible and easy at every major section.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.2 AA for contrast, keyboard navigation, visible focus states, and semantic structure. Respect reduced-motion preferences and avoid color-only meaning where possible.
